LED Thermal
Model.

A parametric engineering analysis project for evaluating the thermal performance of a high-power LED heat sink system.

MATLAB Thermal Resistance Fan Curve CAD

I developed a parametric thermal model for a high-power LED heat sink system, integrating experimental measurements, fan-curve data, and MATLAB-based analysis to evaluate thermal resistance and overall system performance. I also coordinated group progress, contributed to CAD modelling and experimental reporting, and supported comparison between measured results and model predictions for the complete LED lighting system design assessment.

Model Inputs

  • Experimental temperature and performance measurements.
  • Fan-curve data for airflow estimation.
  • Geometry and system assumptions from the LED heat sink design.

My Contribution

I contributed to the MATLAB analysis, CAD modelling, experimental reporting, and group coordination, with focus on connecting model predictions to measured results.
