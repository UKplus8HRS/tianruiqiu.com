Robot War
Build.

A robotics society project focused on turning CAD concepts into fabricated, assembled robot hardware.

Robotics 3D Printing Fabrication Assembly

In the University of Bristol robotics society Robot War project, I worked on model development and mechanical design, then supported the manufacturing path through 3D printing, fabrication, and assembly. The project helped me connect design decisions with real physical constraints such as fit, strength, manufacturability, and iteration speed.

Engineering Focus

  • Model development for robot structure.
  • CAD decisions guided by fabrication constraints.
  • 3D printing, manufacturing, and assembly workflow.

My Contribution

I contributed to model design, prepared parts for 3D printing, and supported physical assembly so the design could move from screen to working hardware.
